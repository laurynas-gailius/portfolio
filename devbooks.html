Internal tools

During my time in software consultancy, I had the pleasure to work on client-commissioned projects as well as designing our own internal tools. From time to time, I would get assigned to the internal tools team to design various applications that increased our productivity, decreased costs, and made us a more transparent partner.

Collaborating with a Project Manager and sometimes another Designer, we kicked things off by understanding the business needs as well as the needs of our colleagues. Followed by execution phase, I would wireframe, prototype, and test the solution, while consulting with a team of Engineers regarding technical feasibility.

I led the design of a time tracking tool used to bill our clients. Also, contributed significantly to designing an analytical tool that provides project status in real-time for our clients. While concluding my role in the internal tools team by delivering designs for the employee allocation planner and revenue forecast tools.

Foros

Environmental services

Foros is an all-in-one platform for buying, selling, and managing private forests. It provides valuable insight and operational recommendations through real-time tree data including maps, reports, and dashboards. The platform helps less savy forest owners to take care of their property and delegate on-site operations to professionals.

As a sole designer, I worked side-by-side with executives to transform their vision for the product into tangible solutions. Based on the information gathered from workshops and research, I wireframed, prototyped, and tested designs with subject matter experts and end-users. Working under tight deadlines, I delivered fully-fledged designs for a customer-facing application as well as a brand new internal tool for managing company's day-to-day tasks.

The client commissioned us to build a robust system, that is capable of dealing with large quantities of data from multiple sources. My task was to design an intuitive, data-focused interface that enables a team of analysts to slice and dice the data and conduct reports.

With established direction and the feature-set of the product, I began to iterate through low-fidelity designs until the required criteria were met. In parallel to the feature work, I also established the application’s visual language, while incorporating the brand’s primary colors and typography.

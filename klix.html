Klix

Fintech

Klix is a payment and shopping service focused on making paying simple and secure for the consumers and the retailers.

It offers direct payments and installment plans in a smooth one-click purchase experience.

My role was to lead design efforts for a number of desktop and mobile products, starting with the re-design of a “buy now, pay later” installment product aimed to improve the user experience and conversion rate of issued loans. I teamed up with a Product Manager and another Designer to address user pain points and elevate the design language through research, user interviews, prototyping, and testing.

I was responsible for creating a new open banking payments solution, as a part of Klix’s one-click checkout experience. I’ve led the project from the beginning by facilitating requirement gathering workshop with stakeholders, discussing user flows, and sketching. I’ve gone through 10+ iterations via rapid prototyping and testing. Shaped and polished UI/UX to get the solution ready for development.

I also got tasked to re-design Klix’s marketing page with the goal to communicate the product purpose more clearly and nudge the visitors toward downloading the app or signing up for a retailer’s partnership. In parallel, the design language was updated and aligned to be consistent with other products.
